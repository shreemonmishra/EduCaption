About EduCaption

EduCaption is an innovative platform designed to make education more inclusive, ensuring learning is accessible to everyone, regardless of physical limitations or impairments. With a strong focus on individuals with hearing or visual challenges, EduCaption provides assistive tools that enhance the learning experience for all students.

Key features include real-time captioning for hearing-impaired learners, screen reading integration for visually impaired students, and a suite of interactive tools such as whiteboards, scheduling, and adaptive learning modules.

By fostering an inclusive learning environment where no student is left behind, EduCaption empowers students to reach their full potential. Its mission is to break down barriers in education, making it more equitable, interactive, and barrier-free for all learners.
