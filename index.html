About EduCaption

EduCaption is an innovative platform designed to make education more inclusive, ensuring that learning is accessible to everyone, regardless of physical limitations or impairments. With a strong focus on individuals with hearing or visual impairments, EduCaption provides a suite of assistive tools aimed at enhancing the educational experience for all students. The platform offers real-time captioning services that allow students with hearing disabilities to follow along with lessons and lectures seamlessly. For visually impaired students, it integrates screen reading technology, ensuring that content is accessible in a more interactive and dynamic way. Beyond just captioning, EduCaption also includes features such as interactive whiteboards, assistive learning tools, and scheduling options, enabling both students and educators to engage with the material in a way that suits their needs. Its goal is to foster an inclusive learning environment where no student is left behind due to physical disabilities, empowering them to reach their full potential. By focusing on accessibility, EduCaption strives to break down barriers in education, making it more equitable,interactive, and barrier-free for all learners, regardless of their abilities.

Our Solution

We've built Educaption, a browser-based platform that provides real-time captions for live classroom lectures. It leverages Al-powered speech-to-text technology and supports multiple Indian languages, ensuring inclusivity and accessibility. It's easy to use, doesn't need extra hardware, and helps students stay connected with the class, improving learning outcomes.We offer captioning, screen reading, interactive whiteboards, and scheduling features to empower teachers and students alike.
